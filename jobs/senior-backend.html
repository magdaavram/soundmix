Senior Backend Engineer

Cluj-Napoca, Romania

We are looking for Senior Backend Engineers passionate about building highly scalable projects and continued development and improvement, that would love to take part in building a global start-up which aims to impact the way millions of people around the world experience live electronic music.

What you’ll work on:

  • analyze and assist on performance improvements to the API;
  • build new features and updates to the existing products;
  • bring improvements to the code and any of the technologies we use;
  • document the code and its components;
  • work closely with the CTO, CPO and other teams.

Who you are:

  • you are passionate about continuously improving the products’ performance and user experience;
  • you speak and write English at a good level;
  • you’re open to learning and implementing new technologies;
  • you love working in teams;
  • you’re looking to be part of a start-up that’s open and transparent, where people are proactive and love what they do.

Required experience:

  • Minimum of 5 years experience in PHP;
  • SQL;
  • Symfony Framework;
  • Linux;
  • REST;
  • PSR;
  • Git.

Knowledge of the following is a plus:

  • Docker;
  • Automated tests;
  • NODE.js;
  • React;
  • Kubernetes;
  • Big data;
  • New Relic;
  • Microservices architecture.

What’s in it for you

Of course, the expected benefits for your level of experience will be there.

After a bit of time of working together, we also offer share options to key people, so that they can feel they really own a piece of the action.

Most importantly we're working on a product that is solving a real need and that will have a huge impact worldwide. It’s really fun to be part of a well funded start-up with global ambitions. We are a team that believes in quality, fun and transparency.
